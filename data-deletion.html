Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: November 16, 2025

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your personal data from Gardenia, please follow these steps:

Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to: raedenjanduque18@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your full name
  • Email address used for login
  • Clear statement requesting data deletion

Option 2: In-App Request

If you're logged into the application, you can contact support through the app's help section.

What Happens After Your Request

  • We will confirm receipt of your request within 48 hours
  • Data deletion will be processed within 30 days
  • You will receive confirmation when deletion is complete

What Data We Delete

When you request data deletion, we remove:

  • Your personal information (name, email)
  • Login credentials
  • Account preferences
  • All associated user data

Note About Third-Party Data

Please note that data stored with authentication providers (Google/Facebook) must be deleted through their respective platforms.

Contact Information

For any questions about data deletion, contact us at:

Email: raedenjanduque18@gmail.com

This data deletion process complies with data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA.
